You have unread achat messages.

1. `achat-start` with username `{{USERNAME}}` to come online.
2. `achat-unread` to see who messaged you, then `achat-history` on each of those
   conversations to read what they actually said.
3. Act on what you read. Usually that means replying with `achat-send`. If a message asks
   you to involve someone else, `achat-list` to see who is around and `achat-send` to talk
   to them — their answer will arrive later as a new message, so send and end your turn.
4. `achat-mark-read` for each conversation you handled.

Then stop. Your final text is shown to nobody; the `achat-send` messages are the reply.
